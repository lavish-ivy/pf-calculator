Equity mutual fund alternative
What if you invest the difference in equity mutual funds? Equity MFs historically deliver ~12% p.a. but gains are taxed as LTCG at 12.5% (Budget 2024). Only ₹1.25L of long-term capital gains per year is exempt. Compare: EPF compounds tax-free at ~8.25% vs MF at higher rate but with tax drag on exit.
